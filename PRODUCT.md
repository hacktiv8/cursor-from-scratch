# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Someone glancing at a wall display — often at a distance, ambient use, no session or interaction. They need the current date and time (Indonesian zones and world cities) without hunting for it.

## Product Purpose

Realtime date and time that is instantly readable. Success is glanceable clarity: the right time is obvious in one look, with no interaction required.

## Brand Personality

Calm, clear, steady — a quiet fixture on the wall, not a campaign or a dashboard.

## Anti-references

SaaS marketing pages — hero stacks, feature grids, CTAs, and card chrome that compete with the clock.

## Design Principles

1. Glance first — every element helps read time at a distance
2. Calm over spectacle — atmosphere supports presence; never demands attention
3. One job — show time; don't sell or explain
4. Hierarchy over clutter — local and world zones stay secondary to readable time
5. Restraint — big type and contrast beat decoration

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Big text, clear contrast, and readable type for distance viewing. Honor `prefers-reduced-motion`. No additional WCAG target beyond these project defaults.
